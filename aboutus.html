Our Doctrine

Palomar Korean Church is committed to the beliefs of the Westminster Confession of Faith and all the doctrinal statements of Reformed Theology. The church is defined by its outlook on Scripture, and it is our conviction that the Word of God is infallible and inerrant; God's authoritative word to His people.
