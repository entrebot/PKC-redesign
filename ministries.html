ROOTS
"There shall come forth a shoot from the stump of Jesse,
and a branch from his roots shall bear fruit."
Isaiah 11:1

Palomar Korean Church's Children's Ministry, also known as Roots Ministry, caters to our beautiful children in our church. This ministry is a healthy, safe environment for kids to come and learn more about God's unconditional love and grace. There are annual festivals and summer Vacation Bible Study to engage the kids in learning more about our beloved Father.

ENCOUNTER
"For I am not ashamed of the gospel, for it is the power of God
for salvation to everyone who believes,
to the Jew first and also to the Greek."
Roman 1:16

Palomar Korean Church's English Ministry (EM), also called Encounter Ministry exists to know Jesus and make Him known! John 17:3 says "And this is eternal life, that they know you the only true God, and Jesus Christ whom you have sent." And we have been called to proclaim his good news through the Great Commission (Matthew 28:18-20)! "And all this is from God, who through Christ reconciled us to himself and gave us the ministry of reconciliation. (2 Corinthians 5:18)" This is the journey we travel together as pilgrims until the return of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ!

OVERFLOW
"'For I know the plans that I have for you,' declares the LORD,
'plans for welfare and not for calamity to give you
a future and a hope."
Jeremiah 29:11

Overflow Ministry is PKC's brand new ministry created about five months ago. This was recently made by Pastor Cho who felt the need to help postgraduate students in their spirtual walk after college. He knows how hard and difficult it is for alumnis to find the same accountability that they are used to from college. His vision is to have this ministry grow more over the next year.

ADULT
"For You are my hope; O Lord GOD,
You are my confidence from my youth."
Psalm 71:5

Palomar Korean Church's Adult ministry exists for the Korean parents and elders to come together in service and worship. This ministry is merely first generation Korean adults who listen to their sermons and sing praises in the Korean language. This is the traditional family-oriented ministry who loves bonding through potlucks and weekly open house bible studies.
