Family Group
"Therefore encourage one another and build up another, just as you also are doing." 1 Thessalonians 5:11

Family Gropu is arranged by the Youth & College Ministry (Encounter). Each Family Group consists of approximately 10-15 members, and meet weekly for fellowship and bible study led by Family Group leaders. Family Gropu holds members accountable for their spiritua relationship with God during the week.
