Praise Team is a group of musically, God-loving members who come together to worship and praise our Father every Sunday service.Practices are held every Saturday mornings. If you have any heart to serve as part of Praise Team, contact Sharon Jeong or Junseok Shim for more information.

Family Group
"Therefore encourage one another and build up another, just as you also are doing." 1 Thessalonians 5:11
What We Do

Family Group is arranged by the Youth & College Ministry (Encounter). Each Family Group consists of approximately 10-15 members, and meet weekly for fellowship and bible study led by Family Group leaders. Family Group holds members accountable for their spiritual relationship with God during the week.
